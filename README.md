# Govinda Pharmacy CRM

An AI-powered Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform built specifically for Ayurvedic clinics and pharmacies.

## Overview

Govinda Pharmacy CRM is designed to modernize traditional Ayurvedic healthcare by providing an all-in-one platform for managing patients, appointments, treatments, inventory, billing, and customer relationships.

The project aims to make digital healthcare management accessible for small and medium-sized Ayurvedic clinics.

---

## Features

- Patient Management
- Appointment Scheduling
- Electronic Treatment Records
- Prescription Management
- Inventory Management
- Billing & Invoicing
- CRM Dashboard
- Follow-up Reminders
- Analytics
- AI-assisted Workflow
